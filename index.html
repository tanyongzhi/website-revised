My Projects

Here are some of the projects that I've worked on.


Book Stop

Book Stop is a web application that allows anyone to search up a particular topic and find the highest rated book for the lowest price. Book Stop will search for the book with the best "bang for your buck", finding a balance between the cost of the book, and the ratings it receives from other readers.

Built with: Nodejs, Express, React

Submission for HackThis 2020


UNIX Operating System

A basic Linux kernel with support for 10 system calls, scheduling, keyboard support, read-only filesystem, exception handling, paging, and i8259 PIC interrupt handling. Also supports a high resolution GUI, audio via SoundBlaster 16 driver, interprocess communication, and a custom debugger.

Built with: C, QEMU, GRUB Bootloader

3rd place in UIUC Best OS Competition 🥉


Makan Tompang

A community app that saves our energy in ordering take-out. Makan Tompang is an app that connects users who want a food delivery (buyers) with users who are already at restaurants (couriers). The app considers the route that the courier would take from the restaurant to their homes/destinations, and suggests buyers whose locations are on their route, in order of how much of a detour they would have to make to get to those buyers.

Built with: Python, Sketch, Flask

Submission for BoilerMake 2020


VR Ant Man Simulator

A Virtual Reality game inspired by Marvel's Ant-Man Simulator. The player can grow/shink between 3 different sizes and explore a house by completing a series of tasks/puzzles. The game was created using Unity and tested/played with the Oculus Rift S.

Built with: Unity, C#
